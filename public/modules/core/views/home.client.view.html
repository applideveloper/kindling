kindling - the less sucky Tinder

Tinder is awesome. But kindling is awesomer. What's so awesome about it? Well, it sucks less. That seems to be the catch-phrase, anyway. But I guess you'll just have to give it a spin yourself in order to see what all the hurrah is over.

Login Register



Swipe-Undo!

Everyone knows that heart-stopping moment when you accidentally swipe-left and forever rid yourself of the opportunity to meet your soulmate. With Tinder, the only fix was to delete your account and start over on the match-making process. Not anymore - kindling allows undoing your last swipe, be it left or right!

Available everywhere!

There's no more need to download an app just to get started. kindling is available anywhere you can find a browser. And yes, that means it's even available on your desktop at work.

Location Modes!

Location modes allow kindlings to indicate that they are traveling and thus only in the area temporarily. Other kindlings have the option of filtering people who are only passing through.
